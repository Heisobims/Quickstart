Quickstart Capital Management is a Syracuse, NY-based investment adviser, serving Onondaga, Oswego, Oneida, Cayuga, and Madison counties and the rest of Central New York. Our mission is to create a clear definition of financial success for our clients and design understandable but effective plans to get there.

Who We Are

We are a fee-only financial adviser, meaning that we are your trusted investment fiduciary. Unlike some other advisors, our fee-only model means that we don’t earn commissions for selling you certain financial products. Our only priority is helping you to find success.
The Quickstart Capital Management team is made up of experienced and knowledgeable financial and retirement planning professionals. We are not only committed to providing the best possible financial advice, but we also take pride in offering best-in-the-business service. Our team looks forward to helping you achieve your financial goals.
