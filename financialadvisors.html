Financial Advisor Team Members

Quickstart Capital Management, LLC is a fee-only, independent, Syracuse-based SEC-registered investment advisor. We work with individuals and organizations in the central New York Region to help them make sound financial decisions. Our main areas of specialty are retirement plan consulting, individual wealth management, and third-party asset management.

Who We Are

What Our Values Are

At Quickstart Capital Management, LLC, every decision made by our financial advisor team members is guided by our commitment to our clients. This dedication is best described in two values: service and integrity.

Service

We are committed to helping our clients achieve financial success. Whether an individual, family, or business, each client can expect us to work hard to understand its goals and expectations. Then, using that insight to design a thoughtful and effective financial plan.

Integrity

Transparency and honesty are top priorities for us. We are a fee-only firm, meaning that our only priority is the success of our clients. In every decision and suggestion that we make, we strive to be as clean and open as possible about our thinking and expectations.
