Who We Are

QuickStart Capital, LLC is in West Genesee Camillus NY. Founded in 2007, we specialize in retirement plan consulting, individual wealth management, and 3rd party asset management.
As a fee-only adviser, Quickstart Capital acts as our client’s trusted investment fiduciary.
Our mission is to define what financial success means to our clients and design a plan to get there.

Partnership with Perigon Wealth Management

We are excited to announce Quickstart Capital Management's new partnership with Perigon Wealth Management, a top ranked, $7 billion national investment and financial planning firm. While the team at Quickstart Capital remains the same, we anticipate this partnership will allow us to deliver enhanced services, investment solutions, and enhanced efficiency to help our clients toward meeting their financial goals. As a client, your loyalty means the world to us. We look forward to continuing to serve you as a proud Perigon Partner.
