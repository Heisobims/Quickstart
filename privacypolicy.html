Privacy Policy

Quickstart Capital (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or communicate with us.

This policy is written in accordance with applicable laws, including the New York State Consumer Protection laws, the New York SHIELD Act, and other applicable U.S. privacy regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:
A. Personal Information
Information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms, email, consultations, or subscription services, including:
Name
Email address
Phone number
Mailing address
Financial and personal goals (if shared during consultations)
B. Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our website, we may collect:
IP address
Browser type and device
Pages visited and time spent on site
Cookies and tracking technologies


2. How We Use Your Information
We may use your information to:
Respond to inquiries and provide services
Personalize your user experience
Schedule consultations and follow up
Send newsletters or updates (you may opt-out anytime)
Improve our website and services
Comply with legal obligations
3. Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:
With trusted service providers (e.g., email systems, payment processors) who help us operate our business and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
To comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful requests by public authorities.
If we refer you to a licensed professional, such as a registered investment advisor, attorney, or CPA, we may do so with your explicit consent.
4. Data Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data, including SSL encryption, secure storage, and access control. While no method is 100% secure, we strive to follow best practices under the New York SHIELD Act to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.


5. Your Rights and Choices
Under New York and federal law, you have the right to:
Access the personal data we have about you
Correct inaccurate or outdated information
Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal exceptions)
Opt out of marketing communications
Withdraw consent at any time for data previously shared
To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@quickstartus.com.
6. Cookies and Tracking
We may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze traffic, enhance site performance, and personalize content. You can modify your browser settings to disable cookies, though this may affect your site experience.


7. Children's Privacy
Our website and services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.
8. External Links
Our website may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Please review their policies before providing any personal information.
9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or business changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date." Continued use of our website signifies your acceptance of any changes.
