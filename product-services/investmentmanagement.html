What Is Investment Management?

Investment management is an arrangement in which a financial advisor actively oversees and handles financial assets and other investments owned by a client. You can think of it as being a comprehensive management service for your investment portfolio. It includes planning, buying, selling, and other duties to help ensure that your financial goals are met.

We don’t just manage specific investments in your portfolio; we manage the portfolio itself. This means that your goals, risk tolerance, and priorities all play important roles in how we approach the job. We also develop comprehensive short- and long-term strategies to deliver the right asset allocation for your needs.

As a Registered Investment Advisor, we are your fiduciary. That means we have a legal and ethical responsibility to always act in your best financial interests. You can be confident that we will only make well-informed and research-backed decisions when managing your investments.

Why Investment Management Is Important

Using an investment manager can be beneficial in many different scenarios. Overall, it is a relatively hands-off approach option for you. These are a few examples of situations that may be best served by investment management:

  • You prefer to have an expert plan and manage your investments for you.
  • You want to ensure your investment portfolio is always being looked after by a knowledgeable professional.
  • You don’t have the time or interest to manage your own finances directly.
  • You are dealing with relatively complex finances such as inheritance, retirement planning, tax strategies, and similar situations.
  • You have had a major life event that has changed your financial situation, and you need some more comprehensive help with your money.

In short, investment management is important because it helps you get the best results without requiring you to do all the careful research yourself.
