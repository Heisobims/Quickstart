What Is Financial Planning?

Financial planning services take a high-level look at your finances to help create a comprehensive plan for the future. The result should be a documented financial plan that describes your current financial situation, your long-term goals, and the steps to help you get there.

Some of the factors included in a financial plan include your current net worth (assets minus liabilities), your cash flow, and your priorities. Since these can change as new developments in your life occur, your financial plan should also change. You can consider a financial plan to be a “living” document because it needs to grow, change, and adapt just like you do.

Typically, financial planning refers to creating a whole-life financial strategy. It can cover planning for today, retirement, end of life, and more. We typically encourage clients to work with one financial planner (or team of planners); however, some prefer to work with us on only one aspect of their financial lives.

An effective financial plan should always be tailored to the client. Everyone’s goals are different, as are their risk preferences. When the Quickstart Capital Management team works with you on a financial plan, we will carefully consider your tolerance for risk as well as your long-term expectations. We will provide you with a clear evaluation of what is possible and produce a thorough strategy for your finances.
