About Us

Not All Advisors Are the Same

Quickstart Capital Management is a fee-only advisor. This means our revenue comes solely from the fees we charge clients rather than commissions for selling certain financial products (many advisors have a fully or partially commission-based model). In other words, your financial success and satisfaction are our only priorities. We only make recommendations in your best interest because our success is based solely on your success.
